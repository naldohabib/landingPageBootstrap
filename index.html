F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do I do if I want to get the discount offered?

    Login to your account, select the deal you want, and click the "BUY" button, then click the "PAY" button. Make a payment according to the payment method you chose.

  • Why should you order at a staycation?

    All prices listed on the StayCation website or application are final prices (including tax). The products offered also vary, from hotels, plane tickets, train tickets, tour packages, to activities. With many choices of payment methods, your every transaction is guaranteed to be safe. Don't worry if you run into problems during the booking process, because your Personal Travel Assistant is ready to help.

  • I can't find the tour package I want to buy, can I make a special tour request?

    Yes, of course! You can submit your desired tour request via the "My Trips" section of the tours page. You can also ask the Personal Travel Assistant by email at cs@staycation.com, call +62821 7564387, Whatsapp at +62821 7654388 or use the live chat feature on the StayCation website or app.

  • Do I need to bring a physical copy of the entry ticket when I arrive at the attraction?

    Even though you can save it on your phone and show it to the entrance attendant, Mister suggests printing it anyway.

  • If I log in using my Facebook account, where will my voucher or e-mail ticket be sent?

    Hotel vouchers, tours & activities, as well as plane and train tickets will be sent to the e-mail address you used to log in. If you log in using your Facebook account, vouchers and tickets will be sent to the e-mail address you used to create your Facebook account. Therefore, make sure the e-mail address registered to your Facebook account is active. If it is not in use, please change your e-mail address first on your Facebook account. You can also log out of Facebook and use your active e-mail address to log in to Staycation.

  • Can I return or cancel my order?

    Yes, if you haven't made a payment. If the payment has been processed and the coupon has been issued, the order cannot be canceled.
